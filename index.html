Relative price ratio view (A / B)

This chart now plots one item directly against another as A ÷ B over time (for example, gold priced in bitcoin = gold ÷ bitcoin).

  • Above 1.00: one unit of A costs more than one unit of B.
  • Below 1.00: one unit of A costs less than one unit of B.
  • Rising line: A is appreciating versus B.
  • Falling line: A is weakening versus B.

Use this as a clean cross-asset lens when denominator framing would otherwise cancel out.

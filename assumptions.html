Assumptions and caveats

  • Denominators include nominal GBP, CPI-adjusted real GBP, gold, average salary, median salary, hours worked at median wage, and bitcoin.
  • Some long-run annual points are interpolated; use “show observed years only” to disable interpolated display.
  • Bitcoin and gold early history is sparse in this dataset and may be volatile.
  • Most values are nominal unless the “Real GBP” denominator is selected.
  • Sources include official, market, and derived series with lineage notes in the app.
